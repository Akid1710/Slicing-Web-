Our Story

Cartoon Network was founded in 2024 with a simple mission: to create a home for the best animated content from around the world. We believe in the power of animation to inspire, educate, and entertain audiences of all ages.

From action-packed adventures to heartwarming family comedies, our carefully curated lineup features shows that celebrate imagination and bring families together. We partner with the most talented creators and studios to bring you content that pushes the boundaries of storytelling.

Today, Cartoon Network reaches millions of homes across the globe, available on major streaming platforms, cable providers, and our dedicated mobile apps. Our commitment to quality animation remains at the heart of everything we do.
